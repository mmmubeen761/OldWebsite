A BRIEF HISTORY

Web & Mobile App Solutions for Your Business

Follow VeriQual's growth as a company over a decade. Our team has helped our clients reach millions of users and raise millions in financing. Watch the video to learn more.

VeriQual is a technology firm that specializes in agile development. We use Ruby on Rails and PHP for web development and iOS and Android for mobile development. VeriQual was started in 2004 and in its 10 years of existence the company has undertaken various large scale projects including Shutl which was recently acquired by eBay, a print management company acquired by Xerox, Africa Media Online, Samsung, Abbott Pharmaceuticals and more

Values VeriQual believes in developing robust and maintainable technology which will scale to serve our client needs. Time and again, we have helped create a platform for our customers to thrive in the long run. Companies such as Bookingbug, Menuspring and Shutl have raised venture capital financing based on software we helped develop

Achievements VeriQual has created over $500 million in value for its clients. Our customers have benefited from technology we have developed for them through new customer acquisitions and raising venture financing. Several of our customers have also been acquired by larger companies. VeriQual has completed over 500 projects including 50 long-term projects that have run for a period of 3 years or longer.

We have been extremely fortunate to have worked with some of the industry's most innovative minds on creating some amazing products. VeriQual's focus has always been on developing well-engineered software that results in value creation for our clients.
